# Atlas Visibility Policy

The Atlas may contain public, internal and archived records.

## Public

Use public visibility for information that can be safely published, accurately described and kept current.

## Internal

Use internal visibility for operational notes, sensitive context, draft relationships or information that requires steward review before publication.

## Archived

Use archived visibility for companies, partnerships, ideas or records that are no longer active but remain part of institutional memory.

## Rule

Never imply ownership, control, partnership or operating authority unless the relationship is accurate and documented.
