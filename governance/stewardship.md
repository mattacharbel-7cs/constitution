# Stewardship

Stewardship is the duty to preserve, improve and transfer the philosophy of 7CS.

## Responsibilities

- Protect the core philosophy.
- Keep institutional records accurate.
- Preserve decisions and their reasons.
- Strengthen the ecosystem without weakening the name.
- Leave the institution stronger for the next steward.

## Rule

A steward may evolve the expression of 7CS, but must not weaken the philosophy that gives it meaning.
