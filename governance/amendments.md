# Amendments

The Constitution may evolve when evolution strengthens clarity, continuity or stewardship.

## Amendment Record

Every meaningful change should record:

- Date
- Steward or proposer
- Files changed
- Reason
- Expected impact
- Approval status

## Rule

Amendments must clarify, preserve or strengthen the philosophy. They must not erase institutional memory.
