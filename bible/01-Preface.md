# Preface

Every generation inherits the work of those before it.

Some preserve it.

Some expand it.

A few transform it into something greater than themselves.

7CS was never intended to become a collection of companies.

It was created to become an enduring institution.

An institution built upon ideas rather than industries.

Upon principles rather than products.

Upon stewardship rather than ownership.

This Constitution exists for one purpose:

To preserve the philosophy that binds every venture, every partnership, every investment and every future generation together.

Companies may change.

Markets may evolve.

Technology may disappear.

The philosophy must remain.

That responsibility belongs to every steward entrusted with 7CS.
