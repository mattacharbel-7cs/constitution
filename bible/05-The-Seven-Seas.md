# The Seven Seas

Across the Seven Seas means the institution is never limited by geography.

7CS begins from Lebanon, but it is not confined to Lebanon.

Its ambition is international.

Its mindset is borderless.

Its duty is to connect markets, people, technology and opportunity wherever meaningful value can be created.
