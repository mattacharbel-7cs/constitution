# The Steward

The founder is not the permanent center of the institution.

The founder is the First Steward.

Every generation becomes responsible for carrying the philosophy forward.

A steward does not merely inherit.

A steward protects, improves and transfers.

The duty is simple:

Leave 7CS stronger than you found it.
