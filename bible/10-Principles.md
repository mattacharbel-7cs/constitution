# Principles

## Long-Term Thinking
Build what can endure.

## Stewardship
Protect the philosophy before the asset.

## Engineering Excellence
Respect precision, systems and execution.

## Integrity
Never create value by destroying trust.

## Quiet Confidence
Let results speak louder than claims.

## Adaptability
Preserve the philosophy. Evolve the expression.

## The Next C
Every generation must build what its time requires.
